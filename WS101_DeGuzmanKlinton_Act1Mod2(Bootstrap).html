I am Me

On 20 October 1993, my journey on so-called "Life" began. My name is Klinton F. De Guzman, the fifth child among the seven children of Franco De Guzman and Angeles Ferrer. I was born in Bacoor, Cavite. Our permanent address is in Linoc, Binmaley, Pangasinan. On the other hand, our recent address is in Baybay Lopez, Binmaley, Pangasinan. I can speak fluent Tagalog, Pangasinan, and a little bit of English. I strongly believe that "Life comes and go but legacy lives forever."

I finished studying my pre-school on 2000 back in Bacoor, Cavite. After moving into Dagupan, Pangasinan, I finished elementary on 2006 in Dagupan City, Pangasinan. Also, after graduating from elementary, we moved to Binmaley, Pangasinan where I finished my high school on 2010. Some of my achievements in high school, both in academics and extra curricular activities are:

  • Academics Excellence Award;
  • 1st Honorable Mention Award;
  • Member of BCS Men's Basketball Championship Team 2010;
  • Member of Binmaley Catechist Batch 2010; and
  • 3rd Placer in Balagtasan: Buwan ng Wika 2010.

Finding passion in life is very hard. I am glad to call myself as one of the few who has passion in Math. I resume studying and started my college life on 2019. I am now in my 3rd Year taking up BS Mathematics Major in Computer Information Technology. I am very fond of computers ever since my childhood. Computer games like Counter-Strike (Half-Life) made me fell in love with computers in an instant. From that moment on, the love I felt with computers grew even more, and that leads up to now. Some of my other hobbies besides computer games are:

  1. playing basketball;
  2. watching Netflix; and
  3. singing karaoke on Youtube.

I always compare myself into a sponge. Like a sponge, I aways see to it that I absorb some lessons that every day gives me and use it for the betterment of myself the next day. I also believe that doing my best in every aspect of life to be the best version possible is always the true purpose of being alive.
